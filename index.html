Our Self-Defense Courses

Individual Course

  • One-on-one training tailored to your goals and fitness level
  • Personalized techniques based on your body type, experience, and comfort
  • Flexible scheduling — sessions by appointment
  • Ideal for those who prefer privacy or have specific needs
  • Focus on building confidence and mastering core self-defense skills at your pace
  • Great for beginners or those recovering from injury
  • Can be held at our studio or at your location

Group Course

  • Train in a supportive, empowering small-group environment
  • Structured curriculum over several weeks ( 6 sessions)
  • Learn techniques through partner drills, games, and real-life scenarios
  • Encourages teamwork, confidence, and situational awareness
  • Lower cost per person — great value
  • Energizing and fun atmosphere
  • Great for friends, families, or colleagues who want to learn together
